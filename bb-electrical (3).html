About Us

Your Local South West Electricians

Born and bred in Bunbury, B&B Electrical Contracting was built on a passion for quality work and a commitment to our local community.

Our Story

B&B Electrical Contracting was founded right here in Bunbury by locals who know and love the South West. We started this business with one clear goal — to build lasting relationships with quality clients who deserve honest, professional electrical work done right the first time.

We believe that great electrical work isn't just about getting the job done — it's about doing it safely, neatly, and to a standard we're genuinely proud of. Whether it's a small domestic repair, a full new home fit-out, a commercial installation, or a solar system, we bring the same level of care and attention to every single job.

From fault finding and switchboard upgrades to data cabling, outdoor lighting, air conditioning circuits, and everything in between — if it's electrical, we do it. No job is too big or too small, and every client matters to us. We're Bunbury locals, and we're here for the long haul.
